How We Are Different

Who Should Consider Us

We specialize in chronic pain and outpatient orthopedic physical therapy. Whether your pain is new or old, we work improve your quality of life

We Use Our Hands

Yes, we need to use our hands in order to perform many of our services. This may be the first time you have had such an experience with a physical therapist or with any provider.This is not massage therapy, either. Our techniques are backed by research and specialized training.

We Get Results

We do not want to cover up your pain. Instead, we are looking for what causes it.We find a way to reduce the pain and get you back to the things you enjoy doing, from walking to taking a walk. We work with you to listen to your body and learn how to trust what it tells you.
